About Code: Louisville

Code: Louisville was a program created by the Louisville Metro Government to help expand and bring new people into the Web Development field.
We have many jobs here in Louisville that need Junior Developers (and the more experienced) to assist with projects. Now, designing a web page may not be your cup of tea.
However, maybe back-end development is. You can help maintain and secure DataBases, help integrate data from applications, build server-side software, work with security settings and defend against hackers.
The possibilities are endless.
